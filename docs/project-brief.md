## Project Concept

### What are you building?

This website stands for the final course project for the Advanced Web Design subject. A fully built portfolio template, ready to be used by any creative professional or designer, developed with a clean and bold aesthetic and a strong focus on usability and performance.

### Who is it for?

The template has been exclusively designed under the idea of "from creatives to creatives".

### Why does it matter?

This template is designed to serve as a practical and customizable tool for creatives of all kinds. Simple yet thoughtfully crafted, it combines a clean structure with a unique, analog-inspired style. Every detail has been considered, ensuring a responsive experience, intuitive navigation and storytelling, and a clear strategy to stand out among the competence.
